About Doghouse

Doghouse is here to unite all dog-themed communities 🐾 and welcome everyone else into our Doghouse. It’s not just a token—it’s a movement to bring people together.

This is where the party’s at 🐾. Doghouse is creating a lively, inclusive space for communities to connect, ideas to grow, and trust to thrive. Whether you’re a proud member of a dog-themed community or just discovering the meme space, Doghouse is where you belong.

Why Pump Fun?

We chose Pump Fun because we believe in its potential to be more than just a platform for struggling projects. While it has often been associated with ventures that fail to thrive, we’re here to rewrite that narrative. By taking this route, we aim to lead by example, proving that a well-executed, community-driven launch can bring success and credibility.

Our mission goes beyond just launching a token; it’s about restoring faith in Pump Fun and demonstrating that quality, trust, and innovation can prevail. Together, we are determined to make Pump Fun great again and set a new standard for what’s possible.
